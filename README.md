# Bright-TV
OVERVIEW *In this you will find the Analysis to improve viewership of Bright tv perfomance, as well as the insights and action plans.

AIM *This aims at the trends and behavious of viewers across all demographics. This will aid the CEO and team to make informed desicions based on the analysis provided.

INSIGHTS *The viewers of Bright TV generally prefer short duration content of 15 minutes or less.
*Weekends have the highest viewership than weekdays.
*Gauteng followed by Western Cape have the highest user base.
*Young adults who are mostly male between the ages 20 and 39 dominate the viewing times.
*Supersport Live Events is the most watched channel.

RECOMMENDATIONS *Focus on targeted marketing, strategically move some of the least watched programmes to days and times with most viewers.
*Prioritize the short content of 15 minutes or less to retain users.
*Use regional and demographic advantage to gain more users, people will watch what they relate to.
